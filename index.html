Power of Adhocracy

An open and free conference on hacktivism and technical means for activism.
Tuesday evening, October 25th. Stockholm, Sweden.

The conference is an unofficial warm up for the conference on Internet and democratic change (Swedish), organized by SIDA on the following day. How can we use technology to ensure democracy and free speech? How about literacy, women's rights or sustainable consumption; can hacktivists contribute? The conference is now over. Thank you to every one for talking & attending!
